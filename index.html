Determine Your Hat Size

Wrap a measuring tape around your head. Press firmly, but not too tightly so it is flush against your skin. It should rest about 1/8 inch above your ear. Once you have the measurement, reference the hat sizing chart below to find the correct size. If you are in between sizes, you can insert sizers beneath the sweat band for a better fit.
